How to choose a mobile phone?

  • Decide on the class of your smartphone.
  • Decide which operating system you need.
  • Examine the display specifications.
  • Pay attention to the parameters of the camera.
  • Don't forget about the technical stuffing of your smartphone.
Decide on the class of your smartphone.

Budget smartphones are devices that don't have super-powerful features. They have a limited amount of RAM, a small screen resolution and a poor camera quality. However, you can call, chat in instant messengers, surf the Internet, take an amateur photo or video without any problems. The strong point of fashion smartphones is design. Often, models in this category are presented with different body colors, have large bright screens.

Decide which operating system you need.

The downside to Android is vulnerability. Due to the openness of the source code, it is easier for hackers and viruses to find the "key" and get the personal data of users. In addition, the newest version of the system is installed, as a rule, on models that enter the market with it in the same year. Therefore, it is impossible to make an application that works equally well on all its versions.

Examine the display specifications.

Everything depends on the display: ease of use, picture quality, user's eyesight, etc. The first thing they pay attention to when buying is the type of matrix, on which the image quality depends.Screen resolution is the number of pixels on the screen. The larger the screen, the more resolution you need for a clear picture. Modern devices often have HD (1280x720) and full HD (1920x1080) resolutions. But with such a resolution, the phone will "eat" a lot of energy.

Pay attention to the parameters of the camera.

When evaluating a camera, many tend to look at its resolution or the number of megapixels. It is believed that the more pixels, the better the camera, but this is not entirely true! Of course, this is an important parameter: it is better to buy devices in which the camera resolution is at least 10 megapixels, but the quality of the pictures depends on other characteristics as well.

Don't forget about the technical stuffing of your smartphone.

The efficiency of a smartphone depends on the processor, which has 2 main parameters. The first is the number of cores, the second is the clock speed. And with that, and with the other, the same scheme works: the more, the better. At the same time, do not forget about the quality, for example 4 powerful cores will be better than 8 low-power ones! The operating time depends on the battery capacity. A capacity of 2000-3000 mA is enough for moderate use for 12 hours. If you use the device a lot and actively, then you should choose a more energy-intensive battery.
