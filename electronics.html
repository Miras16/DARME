Macbook Pro

Apple laptop. It is positioned in the Apple line as a laptop for professional activity.In June 2009, a new version of the MacBook Pro was introduced as a new line of laptops. So far, the screen size of laptops is only 13-inch and 15-inch. In 2012, the series was released with Retina displays, the shell unchanged from 2007 — the resolution of the display increased 4 times, and Sandy Bridge processors were replaced by the more advanced Ivy Bridge, including support for USB 3.0 and HD Graphics 4000. In the 15-inch model, the manufacturer of the video chip is nVidia again, and now the models are equipped with 650 GB of GDDR1 memory with 5m graphics.
